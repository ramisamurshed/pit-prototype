Culture Buddy Platform

Welcome!

We would like to foster inter-community help and well-being by offering you the possibility of both seeking language help and attending cultural events.

You will be able to consult cultural events occurring around your area. You will notice that the description for each event not only includes practical event details but also will show members of the community who have volunteered their time and expertise to help with a particular language service. If you see an event and/or language service that you like, feel free to register!
